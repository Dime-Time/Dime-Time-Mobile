Dime Time Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 18, 2025
Last Updated: September 18, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Financial Information

  • Bank Account Data: Account numbers, routing numbers, transaction history (via secure banking APIs)
  • Transaction Information: Purchase amounts, merchant names, round-up calculations
  • Debt Information: Credit card balances, loan amounts, payment schedules
  • Cryptocurrency Data: Purchase amounts, portfolio balances, transaction history

Personal Information

  • Identity: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth
  • Address: Home address for identity verification and compliance
  • Authentication: Replit account information (when using Replit login)

Technical Information

  • Device Data: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage Analytics: App interactions, feature usage, performance metrics
  • Location: General location for security and compliance purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

Core Services

  • Round-Up Processing: Calculate and collect spare change from transactions
  • Debt Payments: Process weekly ACH payments to reduce your debts
  • Crypto Investments: Execute cryptocurrency purchases per your settings
  • Financial Tracking: Provide insights and progress tracking

Security & Compliance

  • Identity Verification: Comply with financial regulations and prevent fraud
  • Account Security: Monitor for suspicious activity and protect your funds
  • Regulatory Reporting: Meet legal requirements for financial services

3. Information Sharing

Service Providers

We share data with trusted partners to provide our services:

  • Sila Money: ACH processing and payment services
  • Banking APIs: Account connectivity and transaction data
  • Coinbase: Cryptocurrency purchase and portfolio management
  • Replit: User authentication and account management

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, legal process, or to protect our rights and users.

We Never Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or financial information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

  • Encryption: All data transmitted using industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL)
  • Secure Storage: Financial data stored with bank-level security measures
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication requirements
  • Regular Security Audits: Ongoing security assessments and improvements

5. Your Rights & Choices

Access & Control

  • View Your Data: Access your personal and financial information in the app
  • Update Information: Modify your profile and preferences anytime
  • Delete Account: Request account deletion and data removal
  • Opt-Out: Disable specific features like crypto investments

Communication Preferences

  • Notifications: Control app notifications and email communications
  • Marketing: Opt-out of promotional communications

6. Beta Testing

During beta testing:

  • Test Data: We may use test/simulated data for safety
  • Feedback: Your feedback may be collected to improve the app
  • Limited Features: Some features may be disabled or simulated
  • Data Retention: Beta testing data may be retained for development purposes

7. Children's Privacy

Dime Time is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we discover we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email. Your continued use of Dime Time constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. International Users

Dime Time is currently available in the United States. If you access our service from outside the US, you acknowledge that your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States.
